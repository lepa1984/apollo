Keeping Your Most Critical Assets Safe as Threats Evolve From 2D to 3D

The spread of affordable, easy-to-use unmanned vehicle technology powers an ever-growing class of threats to global safety and security. ApolloShield keeps the world’s most critical and threatened assets secure from the rapidly emerging plethora of aerial threats by making airspace security robust, attainable, and painless to understand and use.

About ApolloShield

ApolloShield is a team of top-notch SIGINT, EW, Artificial Intelligence, special forces, and perimeter security experts, developing world-renowned counter-drone solutions.

Founded in 2016 by military-trained engineers from Israel’s world-renowned SIGINT units, ApolloShield designs and deploys C-UAS systems that detect unwanted UAVs (drones), deny their entry into secure airspace, and locate their remote operators in real-time, thus enabling security personnel to catch and stop unauthorized aerial intruders.

ApolloShield collaborates with customers and partners in more than 43 countries, and its products now protect critical assets in a variety of industries such as Oil, Gas, and Energy Utilities, National Infrastructure, Stadiums, Prisons, Public Events, Airports, Military / Defense, Commercial HQs, VIP / Head of State residences, and more.

ApolloShield is globally renowned for its leading expertise in the fields of radiofrequency sensors, signal detection library, and integrations, achieving top international benchmark rankings in tests performed by government customers across multiple tier-1 countries.

Ever since our early days, ApolloShield’s team has always made it a special priority to work with highly knowledgeable experts on the customer’s or integrator’s side and create end-to-end solutions together that are cost-effective and excellent, both technically and operationally. Our unique experience through these unique partnerships, collaborations, and customizations, has earned us a special reputation as a trustworthy and reliable partner for our customers’ most mission-critical systems.

Our Vision

Improve the security of the world’s most threatened assets by making airspace security robust, attainable, and painless to understand, deploy, and use.

Our Mission

ApolloShield partners with End Users, Business, OEM and Technology Partners and leverages its vast experience in homeland security, SIGINT/ELINT, and Electronic Warfare to develop cutting-edge C-UAS products. ApolloShield’s sensors and effectors, combined drone detection and blocking systems, and holistic, multi-sensor/multi-effector solutions effectively protect critical security assets around the world from unmanned aerial threats such as undesired surveillance, unauthorized aerial intrusions, and hostile activities.
